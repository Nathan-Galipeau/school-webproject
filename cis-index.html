Computer Information Systems Department at IUN

Careers After Graduation

Students who graduate with a Computer Information Systems degree typically find work in areas such as:

  1. Network Administrattion
  2. Database Administrattion
  3. Programming
  4. Systems Analysis/Design and Administration

Typical Courses for CIS Students

CIS students take a variety of different courses in order to graduate with a foundational set of skills to succeed in whatever field they choose to pursue.

These courses include, but are not limited, to the following:

  • Introduction to Computers and Their Uses
  • Introduction to Programming
  • Systems Analysis and Design
  • Database Processing
  • Network Administration
